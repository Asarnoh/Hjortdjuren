Einar Ingvarsson, odziany w swe odświętne szaty,

Następnie, sporządziwszy z nich kolumnę marszową pociągnął ów wódz swych podwładnych na okoliczne pole,

Wspólna praca przy obozie przyniosła jednak nieoczekiwany skutek. Wielce mógłby się bowiem zdziwić

Then, when a marching column was formed by them, the leader has taken to team into the nearby fields to inspect how do they fare in a formation.  You do need to know my dear reader, that the warriors who were accompanying the jarl were both young, without experience and knowledge of weaponry.
The common work at the camp has given some unexpected fruit. Any observer could be quite confused to see
