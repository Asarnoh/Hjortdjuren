The dawn of winter has filled nearby villages with warm sunshines that have resulted in numerous thaws.
Together with them some rumours of numorous plunders by some elusive riders have started to deter the travellers.
Hedeby's deputies have thus hired the Golden Deer in order to invesitgate and recon in the nearby forest 
where the plubders had occurred. 

Hence on the dusk of saint Perpetua and Felicity's day we began our journey to invesitigate a waterhole that has been 
hiding in the trees' shadows, a peculiar glade, where common folk used to camp, that seemed to be haunted by starnge powers that seemed to have 
had its life leeched and be full of sand year round, which seemed against the nature itself.
